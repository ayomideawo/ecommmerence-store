Our Story

More Than a Store —
We're a Lifestyle

Founded in 2018, LUXE MARKET started as a small boutique in Ibadan with one mission: make premium products accessible to all Nigerians. Today we serve over 50,000 customers across West Africa, delivering quality and trust with every order.



We believe great products shouldn't require compromising your values or your budget. Every item on our platform is vetted, authentic, and backed by our quality guarantee.

The Journey

From Ibadan to All of Nigeria

What began as a pop-up stall at Dugbe Market grew into one of Nigeria's most trusted premium online stores. Our founder Chukwuemeka Obi launched with 50 products and a bold dream — to give Nigerians access to world-class products at fair prices.

Today LUXE MARKET carries over 5,000 products across five categories, with thousands of 5-star reviews and a loyal community of shoppers from Lagos to Kano, Ibadan to Port Harcourt.

Every package we ship carries not just a product, but our commitment to quality, authenticity, and the belief that Nigerians deserve the very best.
